# Take-Two Interactive Software

Take-Two Interactive Software, Inc. is a leading developer, publisher, and marketer of interactive entertainment. The company operates through its publishing labels: Rockstar Games (Grand Theft Auto, Red Dead Redemption), 2K (NBA 2K, Borderlands, Civilization, BioShock, Mafia), Private Division, and Zynga (mobile games). A Fortune 500 company headquartered in New York City with studios across North America, Europe, and Asia.
